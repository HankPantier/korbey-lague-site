## Positioning & Differentiation

Korbey Lague PLLP positions itself as a year-round accounting partner — not a tax-season-only firm. Their positioning statement reads: *"Your Business Deserves More Than Tax Season."* The firm's reputation in Tyngsborough is built on sustained availability and ongoing advisory relationships, described internally as being "still here in July — not just April."

Key differentiators include:

- **Year-round advisory services** — clients are not handed off after April 15.
- **Tiered bookkeeping packages** (Basic, Standard, Complete) — structured to fit businesses at different stages and complexities.
- **Virtual CFO offering** — CFO-level financial insight and business performance visibility delivered at a fraction of the cost of a full-time CFO hire.
- **Sage Intacct partnership** — the firm implements and supports Sage Intacct, a cloud-based accounting platform, positioning clients for real-time financial visibility rather than end-of-year reporting.
- **Credentialed, multi-disciplinary team** — two CPA partners, one of whom holds the AICPA's Personal Financial Specialist (PFS) designation, supported by multiple MBA-credentialed staff members.
- **Long-term client relationship model** — the firm explicitly frames every client engagement as a long-term partnership.

The firm's tagline is: **"Inspired by your success."**

## Industries Served

### Healthcare Professionals

**Who they serve:** Physicians, dentists, therapists, and specialists — primarily solo practices and small group practices.

**Client pain point:** *"I just opened my practice and I need someone who actually knows how medical practices work, not just someone who handles tax season."*

**Value proposition:** Korbey Lague serves healthcare professionals who need an accounting partner fluent in the structure and cash flow patterns of medical and clinical practices — not a generalist who treats a dental office like any other small business.

### Contractors & Trades

**Who they serve:** HVAC, electrical, plumbing, general contractors, landscaping — typically owner-operated businesses.

**Client pain point:** *"My buddy said I should stop doing my own books. I've got 8 guys now and no idea if I'm profitable."*

**Value proposition:** The firm helps trades-based business owners who have outgrown informal bookkeeping get visibility into real profitability, payroll compliance, and job-level financial performance as their headcount grows.

### Nonprofits

**Who they serve:** 501(c)(3) organizations across social services, arts, education, faith-based organizations, and community foundations.

**Client pain point:** *"We have a grant audit coming up and our books are a mess. We need someone who actually understands how nonprofits work."*

**Value proposition:** Korbey Lague offers nonprofit-specific accounting, including fund accounting, 990 preparation, and grant compliance support — serving organizations that face regulatory and audit obligations distinct from for-profit businesses.

### Service-Based Businesses

**Who they serve:** Marketing agencies, professional services firms, cleaning and maintenance companies, salons, and staffing firms.

**Client pain point:** *"I had a great year but somehow I owe $30K in taxes. I need someone proactive, not just reactive."*

**Value proposition:** The firm works with service-business owners who have experienced the frustration of surprise tax bills and want a CPA relationship built around proactive planning — not after-the-fact compliance.

### Business Startups

**Who they serve:** New LLCs and S-corps across industries — often individuals transitioning from employee to business owner for the first time.

**Client pain point:** *"I'm starting a business and I don't even know what questions to ask. I just know I need a CPA."*

**Value proposition:** Through Business Foundation Services and entity type analysis, Korbey Lague guides new owners through the structural and compliance decisions that matter most at launch — entity selection, accounting system setup, and establishing clean financial practices from day one.
